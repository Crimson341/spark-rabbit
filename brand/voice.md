# Brand voice — Spark Rabbit

## Personality

- Young founder energy without cringe  
- Direct, clear, slightly cocky but kind  
- No corporate speak  
- No “synergy / leverage / disrupt” spam  

## We sound like

> “Your shop is fire. Your Google page is holding you back. I can fix that this week.”

## We don’t sound like

> “As a premier full-service digital solutions partner, we empower stakeholders…”

## Words we use

fast · simple · clean · done · ship · cash · clear · local · results

## Words we avoid

synergy · revolutionary · cutting-edge · holistic · disrupt · best-in-class · synergy again

## Tone by channel

| Channel | Tone |
|---|---|
| Cold DM | Short, human, specific compliment + one offer |
| Sales page | Confident, benefit-first, no fluff |
| Delivery | Professional, calm, checklist energy |
| Content | Behind-the-scenes builds, before/after, lessons |

## Signature lines

- “Simple page. Real leads. Seven days.”  
- “Agencies charge thousands. We ship this week.”  
- “No website? You’re invisible between Instagram posts.”
