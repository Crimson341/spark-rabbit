# Outreach scripts

Keep messages short. Personalize line 1. No walls of text.

---

## Instagram / Facebook DM (local business)

```
Hey [Name] — love what you’re doing at [Business]. I noticed you don’t have a simple website (or the one online looks outdated).

I build clean one-page sites for local businesses in 7 days for $297.

Would you want a free mockup of what yours could look like this week?
```

---

## Shorter version (if first feels long)

```
Hey [Name] — quick one. Your [barber shop / salon / etc.] looks great but I couldn’t find a solid website for it.

I make simple launch pages for local spots in a week ($297). Want me to draft a free sample layout for [Business]?
```

---

## Email (if you find an email)

**Subject:** quick idea for [Business]

```
Hi [Name],

I found [Business] on Google/Maps and checked out your online presence.

A lot of local [barbers/trainers/cleaners] lose customers because their site is missing or hard to use on mobile.

I build a simple one-page site in 7 days for $297 — contact button, hours, offer, photos.

If useful, I can send a rough mockup of [Business] this week at no cost. Interested?

— [Your name]
Spark Rabbit
```

---

## In-person (walk-in)

Hand them a half-sheet or just talk:

```
Hey — I’m [Name]. I help local businesses get a simple website live in a week without agency prices.

If you’re open to it, I can text you a free sample of what a page for [Business] could look like.
```

Get their number or IG. Don’t pitch for 10 minutes in the lobby.

---

## Follow-up (day 3, no reply)

```
Hey [Name] — bumping this once. Happy to send a free mockup for [Business] if useful. If not, no worries either way.
```

One bump only. Then move on.

---

## After they say yes to mockup

```
Perfect. Send me:
1) logo (if you have one)
2) 3–5 photos
3) what you want people to do (call / book / DM)

I’ll send a draft in 48 hours.
```

Mockup is free. Full build is paid. Don’t build the whole site free.

---

## Closing the sale

```
If you like the direction, we can go live this week for $297.
50% to start, 50% when it’s done. Sound good?
```
