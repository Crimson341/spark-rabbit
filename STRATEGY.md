# Strategy — Spark Rabbit Solo Studio

## Positioning

**Who:** Local businesses and solo creators who look amateur online  
**What:** A simple, professional web presence + light automation  
**Why us:** Fast, cheap vs agencies, modern (AI-assisted), founder-led  
**Why now:** Every barber, tutor, realtor, and creator needs a page; most have garbage Google results

### Positioning statement

> For local service businesses that lose customers to competitors with better websites, Spark Rabbit delivers a clean launch page in 7 days for under $500 — without agency timelines or retainers they can’t afford.

## Competitive wedge

| Player | Their problem | Our edge |
|---|---|---|
| Agencies | $3k–$15k, slow | 10x cheaper, 10x faster |
| DIY builders | Overwhelm, ugly results | Done-for-you |
| Freelancers | Flaky, no process | Clear package + timeline |
| Free Canva pages | No conversion focus | Built to get calls/DMs |

We don’t compete with Shopify stores or enterprise.  
We own **“simple page that gets leads.”**

## Business model

```
Phase 1 (Month 1): Services cash flow
  Launch Page → case studies → retainers

Phase 2 (Month 2–3): Productized services
  Templates + fixed packages + faster delivery

Phase 3 (Month 4+): Leverage products
  Free tools → email list → paid micro-product or SaaS
```

**Unit economics (target)**

- Deliver Launch Page in 4–8 hours of focused work  
- Price $297–$497 → effective $40–$100+/hr  
- CAC near $0 (outbound + referrals)  
- Gross margin ~90%+ (time is the cost)

## Go-to-market (zero ad spend)

### Channel priority

1. **Warm network** — friends, family, local businesses you already visit  
2. **Cold DM / email** — Instagram, Facebook, Google Maps list  
3. **In-person** — walk into shops with a one-pager  
4. **Content** — 1 short post/day: before/after, tips, builds  
5. **Referrals** — ask every happy client for 2 intros

### ICP (ideal customer)

- Local service: salons, barbers, tutors, cleaners, trainers, food trucks, handymen  
- Already gets some customers offline or via IG  
- Website is missing, broken, or just a Linktree  
- Decision-maker is the owner (no procurement hell)

### Anti-ICP (skip)

- “Make me the next Uber”  
- People who want free work “for exposure”  
- Enterprise / complex custom apps (too early)

## Pricing logic

| Package | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| Launch Page | $297 | 1 page, mobile, contact form/link, 1 revision |
| Launch + Brand | $497 | Above + logo refresh + 3 social banners |
| Monthly Ops | $199/mo | 2 hrs updates + form checks + tiny fixes |

**Payment rule:** 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. No “pay when I feel like it.”

## Delivery system

1. 15-min discovery call (or voice note exchange)  
2. Collect: logo, photos, offer, hours, contact, socials  
3. Draft in 48 hours  
4. One revision round  
5. Launch + handoff + ask for review + referral  

## Risks & mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| No skills yet | Start with templates; use AI heavily; learn by shipping |
| No clients | 20 outreaches/day until math works |
| Scope creep | Fixed package only; extra = paid add-on |
| Burnout | Cap at 3 active builds at once |
| Imposter syndrome | Charge for outcomes, not perfection |

## 90-day targets

| Milestone | Target |
|---|---|
| Day 7 | Landing page live + 50 outreaches |
| Day 14 | First paid client OR 3 serious proposals |
| Day 30 | $500–$1,500 revenue |
| Day 60 | 3–5 completed projects + 1 retainer |
| Day 90 | $2k+/mo run-rate OR clear pivot data |

## What success looks like

You’re not building a unicorn in 30 days.  
You’re building **proof that strangers will pay you**.

Once you have 3 case studies and $1k+ cash collected,  
you upgrade: better site, niche down, productize, raise prices.
